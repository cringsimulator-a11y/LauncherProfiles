Critical Hotfix: Fabric Login Fix

February 28, 2025
⚠️ This hotfix addresses a critical issue where Fabric versions would fail to launch for Microsoft accounts.

An issue was discovered where the auth_type field was not being read correctly by FabricLoaderLauncher.py, causing all Fabric launches to fall back to legacy auth even for paid accounts.

Update your FabricLoaderLauncher.py to the latest version from the installer to resolve this issue.
